Our many DAILY SPECIALS are a very important part of our menu.  From seared tenderloin medallions to spelt-stuffed acorn squash or pan-seared scallops with risotto cake... our specials shine! If you'd like to know what the specials are today, just call the restaurant at 740-594-3800.

A note about our ingredients:


Milk, cream and yogurt: Were lucky to have Snowville Creamery, a local dairy, supplying us with lightly pasteurized, unhomogenized cream, milk, half-and-half,  yogurt and crème fraiche.

 

Feta: We use a mild domestic cow feta in our spanakopita and salads.

 

Lamb: We serve grass-fed Halal lamb from New Zealand and Australia, raised without growth hormones or antibiotics.

 

Fish and Seafood: We use only fish and seafoods from the Monterey Bay Aquarium Seafood Watch “Best Choice” and “Good Alternative” lists.

 

Produce: Green Edge Gardens supplies us with microgreens year round, and some other vegetables in season. We also purchase from the Chesterhill Produce Auction, Athens Farmer’s Market, and Gibson Ridge Farm when possible.

 

Eggs: We purchase our eggs locally through Green Edge Gardens.

 

Chicken:  Our chicken is naturally raised Gerber cage-free chicken from here in Ohio.

 

Coffee: We buy our coffee from Athens Own/Dawn Chorus.  It’s roasted at the    Silver Bridge Coffee  Company, and ground fresh at Salaam daily.
